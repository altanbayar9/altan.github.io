Overview

The term project is used to provide a framework upon which to introduce principles of web design and development and meet the course objectives. You will be given specifications as if you were contracted to plan, design, and build a prototype website for an organization. In addition, developmental specifications will be given to ensure proper assessment of the application of course content, skills, and technologies. The content is fictitious.
